Cafe Managers

Job Description (Café Manager)

    Purpose:

  • Café Manager is responsible for the successful daily operations of the café.He/she hires, trains, manages, support shift supervisor, Baristas in order to ensure that the team will provide consistent products, clean working environment and the highest level of guest experience.

    • Key Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Demonstrating passion and confidence for the Second Cup Brand

    • Key Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Demonstrating passion and confidence for the Second Cup Brand
  • Honour guests by providing quick, friendly and genuine service
  • Generates guest satisfaction; responds appropriately to guest concerns.
  • Manages and motivates shift supervisors and baristas
  • Develops and implements café policies and procedures
  • Ensures that shift supervisors and Baristas are adhering to all Second Cup Standards and product quality and guest services are always consistent.
  • Responsible for hiring, firing, training and conducting performance reviews
  • Assist Operations Manager with placing orders of supplies required for the café

    • Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:

  • Highly motivated
  • Handle multiple tasks at one time
  • Maintain a positive morale and professional attitude at all times
  • Excellent management and leadership skills
  • Have an understanding of food and beverage operations
  • Must be flexible and able to work days, evening and weekends when called upon
  • Detailed oriented; strong time management, prioritization and organizational skills
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to effectively negotiate and resolve conflict.
  • Communicates with coworkers & management to solve issues and prove quality & service
  • Works with baristas, shift supervisors and management to effectively implement positive change
  • Ensures all baristas and shift supervisors are following cash handling procedures at all times
  • Follow business procedures for handling voids and cash handling mistakes
  • End of shift reconciliation of cash and register records

    • Extra Benefits:

  • Salary + Daily Tip
  • International Certifications
  • Health Insurance
  • Paid Leaves
